Henchmen Modes

Shoot the Henchmen Modes ramp to begin the lit henchmen mode. The lit mode can be changed via the Pop Bumpers or Goldfinger target.

The Henchmen Modes are:

  • Professor Dent (Sinister Summit)
  • Grant (Encounter on the Orient Express)
  • Oddjob (He Blew a Fuse)
  • Fiona Volpe (Femme Fatale)
  • Mr. Osato (Arrange Usual Reception Please)
  • Mr. Wint & Mr. Kidd (Mr. Wint & Mr. Kidd)

Completing a mode awards a Smart Missile.
