Bird 1 Multiball

Shoot the three drop targets in front of the rocket to light the locks. Begin Bird 1 Multiball by shooting up the center lane three times.

At the beginning of Bird 1 Multiball shoot the flashing green shots three times to earn a multiball extender.

Shoot green shots for jackpots. Clearing all jackpot shots reactivates them, and they first become double jackpots, then triple jackpots.

Shoot the rocket when the red lights at the base are flashing for a super jackpot.
