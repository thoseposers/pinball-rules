Modes

There are 24 different modes spread across four different groups. Completing all six in a single group qualifies a wizard mode.

  • Villain Modes
  • Henchmen Modes
  • Q Branch Modes
  • SPECTRE Weapons Countdown Modes

Completing modes awards Smart Missiles.
