Bond Women

The following Bond Women can be collected and act as playfield multipliers:

  • Honey Ryder
  • Tatiana Romanova
  • Pussy Galore
  • Domino
  • Kissy
  • Tiffany Case

Once flashing, shoot the Bond Women inner loop to collect one. Their light will then turn solid. All Bond Women with a solid light are lost at the end of a ball.
