Jetpack Multiball

Left eject shots advance Jetpack Multiball.

Begin Jetpack Multiball by shooting the left ramp once fully lit. The amount of eject and ramp shots required increases for subsequent Jetpack Multiballs.

When the multiball begins, shoot the center lane to lock in a jackpot value. This value decreases over time while not locked. Smart Missiles may be used to complete this shot.

After enough jackpot shots are hit, shoot the left ramp for a double jackpot. Shoot the Dragon Tank Target while the Bond figure passes over it, causing the ball to land on the target above, which activates the Super Jackpot and awards and Add-A-Ball.

Jackpots re-light and this can process can be repeated but this time with the Bond figure being controlled by the flippers.
