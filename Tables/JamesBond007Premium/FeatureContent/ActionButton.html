Action Button

Press the Action Button while it is lit/blinking the following colors for the following perks:

  • Blue (Press) - Smart Missile
  • Flashing Yellow (Hold down) - Add-A-Ball
  • Pink (Press) - 007 Scoring Playfield Multiplier
  • Green (Hold down) - Swipe-A-Ball

If multiple options are available, hitting a flipper will change the color.
