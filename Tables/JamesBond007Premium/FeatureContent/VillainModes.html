Villain Modes

Shoot the Villain Modes ramp to begin the lit villain mode. The lit mode can be changed via the Pop Bumpers or Goldfinger target.

The Villain Modes are:

  • Mr. Henderson (Diamonds of Destruction)
  • Blofeld (Feeding Frenzy)
  • Largo (Disco Volante Demolition)
  • Goldfinger (The Incredible Laser Ray)
  • Rosa Klebb (SPECTRE Island)
  • Dr. No (Trouble in Jamaica)

Completing a mode awards a Smart Missile.
