Skill Shots

Plunge the ball in the following locations to be awarded a skill shot:

  • The flashing MI6 Lane.
  • The flashing MI6 Lane after changing lanes with flippers.

Hold the left flipper and full plunge to attempt a super skill shot on any of the following unique flashing purple shots:

  • Undercover
  • Double "O"
  • Classified
  • Licensed To Kill
  • Eyes Only
  • Top Secret
  • Commander Bond

These will award a Smart Missile.
